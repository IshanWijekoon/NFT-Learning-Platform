Web3 Integration Guide

Learn how to use blockchain wallets with our NFT Learning Platform

Getting Started

Our platform now supports Web3 wallet integration, allowing you to enroll in courses using your blockchain wallet and receive NFT certificates upon completion.

What You Need

  • A Web3 wallet like MetaMask installed in your browser
  • Some ETH or other supported cryptocurrency (for future paid courses)
  • Basic understanding of blockchain and wallet security

Important Security Notice

Never share your wallet's private key or seed phrase with anyone. We will never ask for these details.

Setting Up Your Wallet

Step 1: Install MetaMask

  1. Visit metamask.io and download the browser extension
  2. Create a new wallet or import an existing one
  3. Secure your seed phrase in a safe location
  4. Add some test ETH if you're using a testnet

Step 2: Connect to Our Platform

  1. Navigate to the Course Browser
  2. Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top navigation
  3. Approve the connection request in MetaMask
  4. Your wallet address will appear once connected

Success!

Once connected, you'll see Web3 enrollment options on course pages and can access your wallet dashboard.

Web3 Course Enrollment

With your wallet connected, you can enroll in courses using blockchain technology:

How It Works

  1. Browse available courses on our platform
  2. Click the "Enroll with Web3" button (appears when wallet is connected)
  3. Sign the enrollment transaction in your wallet
  4. Your enrollment is recorded on the blockchain
  5. Access your course content immediately

Secure

Your enrollment is cryptographically signed and verified

Decentralized

No central authority controls your learning records

NFT Certificates

Receive verifiable NFT certificates upon completion

Dashboard Features

NFT Certificates

Upon successful course completion, you'll receive an NFT certificate that proves your achievement:

Certificate Benefits

  • Permanent Record: Stored permanently on the blockchain
  • Verifiable: Anyone can verify the authenticity
  • Portable: Owned by you, independent of our platform
  • Tradeable: Can be transferred or sold (if desired)
Certificate Metadata Example: { "name": "Web Development Fundamentals Certificate", "description": "Completed by [Your Name] on [Date]", "course_id": "123", "completion_date": "2025-09-27", "wallet_address": "0x...", "issuer": "NFT Learning Platform" }

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Wallet won't connect: Make sure MetaMask is unlocked and try refreshing the page
  • Transaction failed: Check you have enough gas fees and try again
  • Enrollment not showing: Wait a few minutes for blockchain confirmation
  • Wrong network: Make sure you're on the correct blockchain network

Need Help?

If you encounter any issues with the Web3 features:

  • Check the browser console for error messages
  • Ensure your wallet has sufficient funds for gas fees
  • Try disconnecting and reconnecting your wallet
  • Contact our support team with your wallet address and error details
